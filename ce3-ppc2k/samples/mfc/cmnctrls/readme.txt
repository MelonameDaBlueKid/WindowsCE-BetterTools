Due to the limited real estate on a HPC device, 
the CMNCTRLS application overlaps the Windows CE Taskbar.
Below is a list of possible ways to access other applications 
or the system menu.

To terminate the CMNCTRLS application
-------------------------------------

Touch the 'X' / OK button in the titlebar, or press ESC / Enter.

To gain access to the Taskbar
-----------------------------

To bring the Taskbar to the foreground, you can point the stylus 
at any visible part of the Taskbar, or press the 'Window' key on 
the HPC keyboard, which displays the Start menu.  

If the visible part of the Taskbar is very small, and the 
CMNCTRLS titlebar is visible, the window can be moved by 
pointing the stylus anywhere in the titlebar and dragging the 
window.  Once you take the stylus away from the screen, the 
window will appear in the new position.

It is not recommended that you use Task Manager to switch to 
another open application.  This may produce problems with 
window painting until the CMNCTRLS application is terminated.
