Microsoft Reader Help

    Use the Reader to read electronic books on your device.

    To open Microsoft Reader, tap   > Programs > Microsoft Reader


    The Microsoft Reader Guidebook provides you with all the information you'll need to use the software. To open the Guidebook, tap Guidebook on the Reader navigation bar. Or, when you're reading a book, tap the book title and then tap Guidebook from the pop-up menu.


    The Guidebook includes a Quick Start, which provides an overview of how to use the software. The How To section gives procedures for using the Reader. Quick Settings lets you adjust viewing options.
