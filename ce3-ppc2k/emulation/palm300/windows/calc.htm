    Use Calculator

      • To open Calculator, tap   > Programs > Calculator.
      • Use the Calculator buttons or the input panel to enter numbers and perform calculations.
      • To clear the current calculation, tap C.
      • To clear the displayed number, tap CE.
      • To clear the last digit entered in a multidigit entry, tap the arrow to the right of the entry box.
      • To clear memory, tap MC.

       

      Store Numbers

        • To store a number, tap the box to the left of the entry box. An M will appear in the box.
        • To add the displayed number to the number that's already in memory, tap M+.
        • To display the number in memory, tap MR.
        • To clear memory, tap MC.
